The Fan Experience, Transformed

FanLog is a permanent, verified archive of your life as a sports fan. Look inside the app.

Sports Identity

Your Fandom, Quantified

Your fandom isn't a simple list of clubs—it is a visual, data-driven instrument custom-mapped to your personal devotion. The Fandom Index rates your team-by-team intensity mathematically, creating a balanced score. It is a completely unique, crisp representation of who you root for, instantly shareable on socials.

📊

Quantified Devotion

Every dynamic brand color, average metric, and custom archetype tagline is calculated from your real sports identity.

The Game Log

Log Every Moment Witnessed

Track how you actually watched the game. Log specific quarters, halves, or innings watched. Missed the third period? Log it honestly. Tag your entries with verified 'In Person' badges via location checks, and keep a searchable, permanent journal of your commentary on every matchup.

Live Chat Rooms

Real-Time Banter, Certified Proof

Every game gets a live, active chat room. Talk banter and game analysis with thousands of fans in team-split layouts. Best of all, checking into the chat room during live game time acts as cryptographic proof of watch, certifying your log entry with a permanent verified badge.

Fandom Wrapped

Spotify Wrapped, for Sports Fans

Go beyond team statistics and track your own performance as a supporter. Discover your live watch streaks, win-loss records while watching, league splits, and team devotion ratios. Do you jinx your team when you watch? Our sports data engine will prove it.
